The "Graphic Design Essentials" course offers a comprehensive introduction to the fundamental principles and techniques of graphic design. Participants will learn essential design concepts, tools, and software used in the field of graphic design. Through a combination of theoretical lessons and hands-on projects.

This course covers topics such as typography, color theory, layout design, image editing, and vector graphics. Participants will have the opportunity to develop their creative skills and apply design principles to create visually compelling graphics for various digital and print media.

Course Outcomes:
  • Develop proficiency in using design software such as Adobe Photoshop, Illustrator, and InDesign.

  • Gain a solid understanding of graphic design principles and techniques.
  • Create visually appealing designs for websites, social media, advertisements, and print materials.

  • Build a professional portfolio showcasing their design projects and skills.
