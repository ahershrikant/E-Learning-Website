The "Introduction to Artificial Intelligence (AI)" course provides participants with a comprehensive introduction to the field of artificial intelligence, covering key concepts, techniques, and applications. Participants will explore the fundamentals of AI, including machine learning, neural networks, natural language processing, and robotics.

Through a combination of theoretical lectures and practical exercises, this course aims to demystify AI and equip learners with the foundational knowledge needed to understand its impact on various industries and society as a whole.

Course Outcomes:
  • Educators will explore real-world applications of AI across various domains, including healthcare, finance, marketing, and autonomous vehicles, gaining insights into how AI is transforming industries and shaping the future.

  • Participants will develop a solid understanding of the basic principles and techniques of artificial intelligence, including its history, components, and core algorithms.
  • Educators will examine the ethical and societal implications of AI, discussing topics such as bias in AI algorithms, privacy concerns, job displacement, and the role of AI in shaping society.

  • Upon completion of the course, participants will be well-positioned to pursue further studies in artificial intelligence or related fields, armed with a solid foundation of AI knowledge and skills.
